Private Residential Inquiry

A house considered as atmosphere, ritual, and long-term identity rather than a simple arrangement of rooms.

  • Location: Seoul
  • Year: 2026
  • Type: Residential
  • Status: In Development

The project begins with the premise that domestic space should support more than utility. It should frame routines, register time, and establish a stable relationship between inhabitant, material, and light.

The proposal organizes the house through measured thresholds, carefully positioned openings, and a restrained palette that emphasizes durability and atmosphere over novelty.
