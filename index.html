Research, judgment, and atmosphere become a single discipline once a project begins to clarify what belongs.

WWWD pays attention to how a place is entered, occupied, and remembered.

The studio is interested in the relation between form and life, how material, atmosphere, and behavior settle into a shared order, and how architecture can hold cultural weight without becoming rhetorical.
